About Us

Our Company

Primeworth Workspace Solutions (P) Ltd., (PWS) is a dynamic & state-of-the-art company, involved in offering Project & Infrastructure management services in Sustainable Technology Solutions.

Our clientele include IT/ITES firms, engineering/production units, Biotech/ Pharma Industries, Real Estate assets (gated communities), international schools, corporate entities, and public infrastructures & utilities.
